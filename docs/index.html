Does Codex Switchboard work with OpenAI Codex?

Yes. It is built for local OpenAI Codex usage visibility and manual account switching on macOS.

Does it upload tokens or account data?

No. Tokens and usage snapshots stay on the local Mac.

Is account switching automatic?

No. Codex Switchboard does not automate account cycling or change Codex limits.
